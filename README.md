# YouTube CTR Evaluator

An AI-powered tool that predicts how many views your YouTube video will likely get based on your channel statistics, video metadata, and thumbnail image.

## How It Works

1. **Upload Your Thumbnail:** Drag and drop your video thumbnail image
2. **Fill in Video Details:** Enter your video title, length, and upload timing
3. **Share Channel Stats:** Tell us about your channel's subscribers, views, and age
4. **Get Prediction:** Our AI model analyzes all this data and predicts your potential views

## What Information You Need

- **Video Title** - What you're calling your video
- **Video Length** - How long is your video (e.g., 10:30 for 10 minutes 30 seconds)
- **Thumbnail Image** - Your video thumbnail (JPG, PNG, or GIF)
- **Channel Subscribers** - How many subscribers your channel has
- **Total Channel Views** - Total views across all your videos
- **Number of Videos** - How many videos you've uploaded
- **Upload Day** - What day of the week you're uploading (0 = Sunday, 6 = Saturday)
- **Upload Hour** - What time of day you're uploading (0-23, where 0 = midnight)
- **Channel Age** - How many years your channel has been active
